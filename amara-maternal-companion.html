Emergency & Safety

Your safety is our priority. Call for help instantly.

⚠️ Go to hospital IMMEDIATELY if you have:

Severe headache with visual disturbances (flashing lights, blurring)
Sudden facial or hand swelling — possible preeclampsia
No fetal movement for 12+ hours after week 24
Vaginal bleeding at any stage of pregnancy
Severe abdominal pain or contractions before 37 weeks
High fever above 38.5°C or difficulty breathing
